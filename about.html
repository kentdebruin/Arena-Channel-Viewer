About

My name is Kent de Bruin. I'm based in Amsterdam, but travel frequently. I am currently working on Futureland. I did projects in a variety of industries including personal computing, artificial intelligence (LLM's), architecture, furniture, government and housing. I work independently on photography projects, and hope to publish my work in book-form one day. Previously, I was the first designer at Sail, founding partner at Studio Kiri (acqui-hired), and co-founder at Acaboo (Sunset) and co-founder at Able (Sunset). I hold a MSc in Digital Design, and a BSc in Business Administration. I also studied Digital Innovation at Hyper Island. I love spending time on Are.na (@kent) researching all sorts of topics. I'm tall (200cm / 6ft7). I dj under the name SIXFOOTSEVEN. For my selections I dig from a variety of genres including progressive, techno, house, ambient, IDM, acid and 90s trance. I'm born on the middle day of the year (non-leap years).
